# LaunchDeck Submission Pack

## Project name
LaunchDeck

## One-line description
An AI workspace that turns rough startup ideas into launch-ready product plans for solo builders.

## Short description for form
LaunchDeck helps solo founders transform scattered product notes into structured PRDs, launch checklists, feature priorities, and weekly execution plans.

## Long description for form
LaunchDeck is a planning-first AI workspace designed for solo builders and indie founders. Instead of acting like a generic chatbot, it helps users move from rough ideas to practical outputs they can actually use. A builder can input raw notes, early product thoughts, market observations, or launch ideas, and LaunchDeck turns them into structured product briefs, positioning summaries, launch plans, and weekly action boards. The product is meant to reduce chaos in the early stage of building and make execution feel more focused and concrete.

## Problem
Solo founders often have scattered notes, half-formed product ideas, and no structured way to move from thinking to shipping.

## Solution
LaunchDeck converts raw input into practical outputs:
- product brief drafts
- launch checklists
- feature priorities
- messaging guidance
- weekly execution plans

## What makes this unique?
LaunchDeck focuses on a narrow but valuable workflow: helping solo builders convert messy early-stage ideas into practical launch plans. Its value is not just text generation, but organizing product thinking into concrete outputs founders can actually use.

## Why should this project be selected?
This project demonstrates a clear AI-native product workflow with obvious value for founders and indie teams. It is focused, opinionated, and built around structured outputs rather than generic chat, which makes it a strong showcase for MiMo-driven planning and generation.

## Architecture concept
